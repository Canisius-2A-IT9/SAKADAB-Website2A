The 3Cs & UP

With each letter signifying “Cooperation, Collaboration, Contribution, Unity, & Participation”, 3CUP is an organization in partnership with the SAKADAB institution. The 3CUP project exists to achieve a mission in which, with a sense of purpose to aim for a common goal, we seek to address the difficult matters experienced within the advocacy and community of SAKADAB. The scheme of the 3CUP project contains three objectives to accomplish and execute — wherein the implementation of each will enable assistance in resolving the identified issues and problems of SAKADAB through amassing finance from different sources of income. Fulfilling the mission of 3CUP ensues the solution to the challenges in scarcity of resources that disrupt the operation and system of SAKADAB, most especially the unmet needs and lacking demands of the SAKADAB community.
The 3CUP project does not only solely exist for the struggles of SAKADAB and the hardships of its members. Besides that, our mission targets to cultivate the awareness of people and share relevant knowledge to others by educating them about the community of SAKADAB and the essential work of the institution. The 3CUP organization, with competence, compassion, and combined efforts, will accomplish spreading important information about the various internal and external aspects of the advocacy which allows individuals to develop a rigorous understanding of SAKADAB.

Significance of Project

The 3CUP project will address the problem of scarce resources in the financial category and healthcare needs of the SAKADAB community. This project will aid in growing an income for the institution by the launching of a livelihood program wherein consumer products produced by the SAKADAB community members will be sold in the selling service of 3CUP. Through this selling service, the 3CUP project will as well serve as a benefit for the individuals of SAKADAB because this project partners with the advocacy mission in which the physical abilities, talents, and skills within the capability of these individuals will be applied in the activity of creating consumer products. Therefore increasing the potential of the SAKADAB community to “improve the quality of living”, “enhance the lifestyle”, and “become self-reliant” just as what the mission of the institution aims which is to open various opportunities in order for the community members to make use of their different capacities. At the same time, we earn an income from this selling service.
Additionally, the 3CUP project will help in spreading awareness, cultivating people’s knowledge, influencing proactivity, and gaining volunteers, participants, fundraisers, offerers, and donors for the SAKADAB institution by initiating social movements or motions such as poster-making, fundraising, offerings, and donations. With these collective procedures, an income will be raised.

The 3CUP project will further extend support in resolving the issues of SAKADAB through including a gardening program All in all, the overall amount of money developed through the implementations of the 3CUP project will be accumulated and transported to SAKADAB. As a result of the assembled sources of total income and its delivery to the institution, the SAKADAB advocacy will gain secured finance. SAKADAB will head towards ensuring economic stability and then proceed with the essential goal to provide important resources that the community needs and demands.

Problem Identification

Our partner institution is SAKADAB.

The significant topics about the profile of our partner institution, SAKADAB, are mainly the financial resources aspects. SAKADAB is experiencing issues related to matters in scarce resources of both categories in finance and healthcare needs. The severe drop in the income of SAKADAB is the current problem caused by the COVID-19 pandemic, which results in the incapability of the institution to acquire and provide the demands and needs of the community; such as essential healthcare supplies, medical tools, and proper equipment. Healthcare is crucial and is the most important need especially for these 35 members who are disabled, abused, and neglected, yet SAKADAB is faced with troubles in addressing this due to their state of economic outcome which is negatively influenced by the difficulties rooted through the global pandemic. Aside from that, not only is SAKADAB unable to adequately obtain vital resources that are needed for the wellbeing and health of their people, but the advocacy will as well likely go through struggles in meeting payment demands on utility bills wherein all consumption of water and electricity (plus other outgoings or expenses) are in critical condition because of an insufficient amount of money in the SAKADAB institution.

Proposed Intervention

1 livelihood program, 1 selling service, 1 gardening program

- To partner with the advocacy mission and target of SAKADAB
- To pair with and thoroughly improve the aim of SAKADAB for the lifestyle and living of its individuals
- To open physical opportunities and discover physical potentials for the SAKADAB community
- To offer physical activities for the members of SAKADAB for a better livelihood
- To introduce a livelihood program
- To launch a selling service
- To establish awareness activities and social motions
- To promote initiative proactivity and influence movements among audience
- To propose the SIP (Science Investigatory Project) along with a gardening program
- To gather supportive people
- To collect amount of money and send it to the SAKADAB institution
